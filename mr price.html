MR PRICE



In the early 1950s, MRP was first computerized by aero-engine makers associated with General Electric and Rolls Royce. Before MRP became commercialized, it was reinvented to use the Polaris program. In 1964, MRP was released to specific individual businesses, being used first by Black & Decker..



Mr Price is a fashion-leading clothing, footwear, cosmetics and accessories retailer that offers on-trend and differentiated merchandise at extraordinary value to ladies, men and kids
